Facilities of NCCS College

Be all rounder at NCCS

NCCS believes in "all-round development makes efficient product" and has installed different sporting sites within its own complex, including both indoor and outdoor games such as basketball, cricket, pool, and table-tennis. Activities like elocution, public speaking, group discussion, debate, and seminars help students develop and stay ahead among their competitors.

High-tech Hotel Management & Cozy Computer Lab

Specialized training kitchens and restaurants, bakery and confectionery labs, housekeeping lab, front office lab, computer lab with the latest technology, and audio-visual facilities are all available for hands-on learning and skill development.

Library at Home and College

NCCS offers both online and traditional libraries with rich resources. Students can log on and access resources from anywhere on the website or borrow a book from the library in person.

Innovation is What Matters

The research wing helps students utilize their knowledge and become leaders. NCCS believes research and development leads to innovation, and students are encouraged to lead with their ideas.
