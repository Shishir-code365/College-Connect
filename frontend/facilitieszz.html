Facilities of NCCS College

Be all rounder at NCCS

NCCS believes that "all-round development makes efficient products." That’s why NCCS has created multiple sporting sites within its campus, including both indoor and outdoor games such as basketball, cricket, pool, and table tennis. Activities like elocution, public speaking, group discussions, debates, and seminars help students develop and stay ahead of the competition in all aspects of life.

High-tech Hotel Management & Cozy Computer Lab

NCCS provides specialized training kitchens and restaurants, bakery and confectionery labs, housekeeping lab and guest room facilities, front office lab, computer lab with the latest technology, and audio-visual facilities to enhance hands-on learning and skills development.

Library at Home and College

NCCS is ahead of others by embracing today’s technology. It offers both online and traditional libraries, rich in resources. Students can access resources from anywhere via the website or borrow a book from the physical library on campus.

Innovation is What Matters

"Research and development lead to innovation." NCCS encourages students to use their gathered knowledge to become leaders. The research wing of NCCS helps students cultivate and realize their innovative potential.

Cafeteria

Need to rush to college? No worries! NCCS has an on-campus cafeteria where you can satisfy your hunger and relax for a moment while enjoying your meal.
