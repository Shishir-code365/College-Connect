Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc.CSIT)

Introduction

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc.CSIT), affiliated to TU, is a four-year degree combining IT and computer courses. It features both lectures and practical project work and prepares students for careers as computer professionals and IT managers.

Program's Objectives

  • Offer intensive knowledge in theory, design, programming, and application of computers.
  • Provide knowledge of hardware and software systems.

Eligibility Conditions for Admission

  • 12-years schooling/PCL in science or equivalent, recognized by Tribhuvan University.
  • Must pass the entrance test conducted by the Institute of Science and Technology (ISOT-TU) with at least 35%.
  • At least second division in 10+2/PCL/equivalent program and all procedures completed.
