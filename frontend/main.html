Welcome to NCCS

National College of Computer Studies (NCCS), college of IT and Management, is a highly professional and experienced college based in Kathmandu which was established in 1999. It educates all students not only to face the challenges of the fast-moving industry but also to perform exceedingly well. NCCS is fully dedicated to providing a quality university curriculum, affiliated with Tribhuvan University.

About NCCS

It comprises of multitalented professionals considered among the best in the industry. NCCS offers a range of programs including Bachelor of Information Management, Bachelor of Hotel Management, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology, Bachelor of Business Management, and Bachelor of Computer Application.
